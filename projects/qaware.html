QAware is a cross-platform application that is changing the way quality assurance and quality control is conducted across many industries. By integrating iBeacon technology with a native iOS app written in Objective-C, QAware ensures that inspections happen when, where, and by whom they are supposed to happen. This was our final project at DBC.
