After a user has signed in, the sign in form is replaced with a very general description of the app.

A user can then look at the user's vehicles in the garage. Each vehicle in the garage shows the last fluid change and part change records.

Navigating to a specific vehicle displays all of the fluid and part changes for the specific vehicle.

App is still a work in progress
